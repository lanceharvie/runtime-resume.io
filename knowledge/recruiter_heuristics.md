# Recruiter Heuristics

- Page one must establish niche fit quickly.
- Responsibilities are weaker than outcomes and scope.
- Embedded resumes should surface MCU, RTOS, protocols, and product context early.
- FPGA resumes should surface vendor stack, devices, toolchains, timing, and verification context.
- DSP resumes should explain the signal or application context, not just say "algorithms".
- Robotics resumes should show controls, perception, integration, and real-world deployment context.
- Long tenure needs progression, ownership, or complexity growth to avoid looking static.
- Generic summaries are usually dead space unless they establish specific market fit fast.
- Keyword stuffing without technical depth reduces credibility.
- Hiring managers care about evidence of systems thinking, not just a list of tools.
