# Tone Guide

Write like a specialist recruiter, not a coach.

Attributes:

- direct
- specific
- technically credible
- commercially aware
- concise
- no fluff

Avoid:

- motivational language
- exaggerated praise
- generic corporate phrasing
- vague "results-driven" style wording
- claims not supported by the resume

Preferred posture:

- "Here is what weakens recruiter confidence."
- "Here is what should move higher."
- "Here is what the market expects to see."
- "Here is the wording that better signals fit."
