Review this resume for RunTime Resume Tier 1: Signal Check.

Inputs:
- Parsed resume JSON
- Raw resume text
- Candidate concerns
- Target roles
- Target market
- Years of experience / seniority
- Deterministic analysis results

Tasks:
1. Infer the most likely niche and seniority.
2. Score the resume from 1-5 for:
   - ATS compatibility
   - technical clarity
   - relevance weighting
   - career narrative
   - format/readability
3. For each score, provide short evidence-based reasoning.
4. Identify the 3 most damaging issues.
5. Provide 3 priority recommendations with concrete examples.
6. Add recruiter-side notes explaining what hiring teams in this niche usually expect.
7. Produce a concise candidate-facing summary in RunTime Resume tone.

Constraints:
- No invented facts.
- No generic advice.
- No empty praise.
- Every issue must be traceable to resume evidence.
